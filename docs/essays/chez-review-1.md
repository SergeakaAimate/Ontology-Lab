# A Critical Assessment of CHEZ: From Apocalypse to Harmony

A cross-disciplinary review of *chez: The Great Restoration* — evaluating its philosophical depth, mechanical innovation, and potential as a model for cooperative intelligence.

## Abstract
This review examines chez not as a chess variant, but as an ontological reconfiguration of conflict itself. It shifts the game’s metaphor from warfare to restoration, from entropy to reintegration, and from zero-sum competition to dialectical co-creation. The analysis spans chess theory, game design, AI, cosmology, and social psychology, concluding that chez offers a cognitive framework for modeling cooperation under constraint—with implications for AI alignment, education, and collective sense-making.
